Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Pixel Perfect is to provide a pixel art editor and creator. It will allow those interested to easily create and export their art.

Audience

Pixel Perfect is intended for anyone in need or interested in creating pixel art.

Dynamic elements

The major dynamic element as part of the site will be the canvas where you will be able to draw and edit art. Along with this will likely be the color picker and brush settings.
